# CivicaID – Inclusive Smart Identity Prototype

CivicaID is a MyKad-powered inclusive service experience designed for elderly citizens, blind users, deaf users, and people with mobility needs.  
Our goal is to enhance Malaysia’s digital identity landscape with adaptive accessibility, multimodal interaction, and real-time assistance.

---

## Features Demonstrated in Prototype

### 1. Adaptive Modes (Auto-Detected via MyKad Profile)
- **Senior Mode**: Large text, high contrast UI, simplified navigation.
- **Blind Mode**: Voice-first commands, vibration feedback, dark high-contrast UI.
- **Deaf Mode**: Visual alerts, LED cues, no audio dependency.

### 2. Emergency Trauma Scan
- Authorized personnel can tap a victim's MyKad to instantly view:
  - Blood type
  - Allergies
  - Chronic conditions
  - Next-of-kin contact

### 3. Accessibility Navigation & Reporting
- Indoor wheelchair navigation
- “Report broken infrastructure” form
- Community-verified accessibility map

### 4. Priority Queue & Ambient Assistance
- Auto-priority queue for OKU/Seniors
- Staff alerts when assistance is needed
